Letter from the Producing Artistic Director

In the 2013-14 season we developed nearly 70 bold and profound new plays, some of which have already been slated for production including Mat Smart's The Royal Society of Antarctica (The Gift Theatre, Chicago), Gabriel Jason Dean’s Terminus (Vortex Rep, Austin), and Carson Kreitzer's Lasso of Truth (Marin Theatre Company, CA; Synchronicity Theatre, Atlanta; Unicorn Theatre, Kansas City, MO).

We also worked on several co-development partnership projects and welcomed many visiting artistic leaders to the Center to get to know our writers’ work. Applications to our fellowship programs were up by 11%, and we also saw increases in membership and audiences attending our public readings.

On the administrative side, we accomplished a great deal of planning work that has prepared us for several large-scale projects in 2014-15, including the launch of our new website with enhanced member tools.

I thank you from the bottom of my heart for your support of the Playwrights’ Center and the 1550+ writers we serve each year. You are helping bring new and innovative work to the American theater!

Jeremy B. Cohen
